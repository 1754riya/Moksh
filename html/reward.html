Pomodoro Feature

25:00

This is an excellent feature that can help users integrate spiritual learning into their daily routine and promote a healthier work-life balance.

  1. Set a timer for 25 minutes and work on your task.

  2. After 25 minutes, take a 5-minute break. An alert will be there to read a Bhagavad Gita shloka with explanation.

  3. After 4 breaks, take a 30-minute break.

  4. Repeat the process until you complete all the tasks.

Good Luck!

Pomodoro Spiritual Break

A Spiritual Productivity Break

With this feature, users can set a Pomodoro timer for a specified interval and receive an alert when it's time to take a break and read a Bhagavad Gita shloka from your website. This can help users stay on track with their spiritual practice and cultivate a more focused and disciplined mindset. Moreover, by reading the Bhagavad Gita shloka during the break, users can refresh their mind and gain a deeper understanding of the divine principles taught in the text. This can help them approach their work and daily tasks with a greater sense of purpose and fulfillment. Overall, offering this Pomodoro feature alert on our website is a great way to promote spiritual learning and support users in their journey towards greater well-being and personal growth.
